About Me

I'm Ibrahim Rabiu, a tech enthusiast with a passion for empowering the next generation of tech leaders. I'm originally from Zaria, Kaduna State, where my love for technology began at a young age.

My academic journey led me to earn a distinction in my Diploma of Computer System Engineering program, which fueled my desire to dive deeper into the world of technology. I'm particularly fascinated by Artificial Intelligence and its potential to revolutionize how we live and work.

I find immense joy in coding and am proficient in Python, JavaScript, and React. Beyond programming, I enjoy exploring the world of data analysis, and my Cisco CCNA certification highlights my understanding of computer networking.

Currently, I serve as the Head of Training at BT Hub, where I combine my passion for technology and education. I'm driven by the opportunity to guide and mentor aspiring tech minds, providing them with the skills and knowledge to thrive in this dynamic field.

My commitment to education is further recognized through my recent achievement of becoming a Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert (MIEE) in 2024.

I believe in the power of continuous learning and constantly seek new challenges and opportunities to grow within the tech industry.
